The D. Y. Patil Institute of Master of Computer Applications and Management (DYPIMCAM), located in Akurdi, Pune, is known for its MCA and MBA programs. It is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The institute focuses on providing quality education and has a strong academic curriculum along with co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

Courses Offered: The main programs offered are the MCA (Master of Computer Applications) and MBA (Master of Business Administration). The MCA program emphasizes both theoretical and practical aspects of computer applications, preparing students for careers in software development, database management, and systems analysis.

Fees: The MCA program fees are around ₹99,771 per year for the general category. Fee structures may vary for different categories, with provisions like EBC schemes for students from financially weaker sections.

Infrastructure: The campus has modern infrastructure, including spacious classrooms, advanced computer labs, a library, and seminar halls, providing a conducive learning environment. The college also has a dedicated placement cell.

Placements: The placement cell offers opportunities for internships and job placements, with top companies like tech firms and multinational corporations regularly visiting for recruitment.

Admissions: Admission to the MCA program is based on the MAH-MCA-CET score. Candidates must have a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields with at least 50% marks for eligibility.
